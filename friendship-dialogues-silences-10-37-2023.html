"Friendship, Dialogues (silences)" 10’37, ©2023

"Friendship, Dialogues (silences)" follows a conversation between ideas (present and missing) in contemporary materialist-film; ie, the (impersonal) documentary film conceiving itself to be an artifact of its past anarchic potential. Wherever there is a limitation imposed, there is always an implied area beyond the limit; this is the nature of the pact between parallels: if ever they should meet, it would be where endlessness terminates; the path is labyrinthine and ruinous.
