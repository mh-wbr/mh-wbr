"Exit Mirror" 11’01, ©2022

Image: one following the next.
Sound: surface, attention, time.

The moving-image rests with an interest in contingency, aimed at the moment, and situated as always ending, always beginning. The specificity of the work asks to not only impart the labor of its appearance, but that it may also be embodied as an image of laboring. Time touches us through the attention we give it. No progress, no sense, no state.

FRACTO: Experimental Film Encounter, Berlin, Germany, 2022 (World Premiere)
