"At Rest/Not at Rest" 22’06, ©2020

From the politics of the slaughterhouse to vanishing in the wilderness: An archive of images harmonize into an ambience of violence, disengagement, and disappearance. The three-frame-loop, or boil, is assisted by the documentary labor of the traced image and its inability to develop. The tone of this work should be read as an object.
